Built for the 1 billion people medicine forgot.

At CARA, our mission is to close the diagnostic gap in underserved communities. Aligned with the World Health Organization’s Universal Health Coverage goals, we believe high-quality healthcare shouldn't be a privilege of geography. By deploying intelligent infrastructure to the "last mile," we ensure that every patient, regardless of their location, is seen and cared for with clinical precision.

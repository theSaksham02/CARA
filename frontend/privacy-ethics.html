The Privacy Policy

Translated into plain english

What we collect

We collect device performance logs and anonymized clinical volume (e.g., "5 screenings performed today"). This helps us keep the platform running.

What we don't

We do not collect names, phone numbers, precise GPS locations of patients, or biometric signatures. These stay encrypted on your local device.

Your Rights

  • delete Right to Erasure: Remote wipe any local data instantly.
  • file_download Right to Export: Move your local database to any other platform.
  • lock Right to Silence: Opt-out of any and all metadata syncing.
